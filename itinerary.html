Our Top Destinations

Goa -3days

Enjoy Goa’s sun-kissed coastline, lively beach shacks and water-sport activities. Great for relaxation by day and vibrant nightlife by night.

  • Morning: Baga/Calangute beaches & water sports
  • Afternoon: Aguada Fort & seaside walk
  • Evening: Local seafood dinner and beach market

Total price for this place/per person: Rs.7500

Special Destinations: Baga Beach, Aguada Fort, Dudhsagar Falls

Jaipur (Rajasthan) -4days

Step into the royal past — palaces, forts, and bustling bazaars that showcase Rajasthan’s unique heritage and crafts.

  • Morning: Hawa Mahal & City Palace
  • Afternoon: Amber Fort (elephant/jeep approach)
  • Evening: Handicraft markets and traditional Rajasthani meal

Total price for this place/per person: Rs.8400

Special Destinations: Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace

Kerala -5days

Slow down with lush hills, tea gardens and serene backwaters — perfect for a houseboat experience and spice-market strolls.

  • Morning: Board a houseboat in Alleppey
  • Afternoon: Munnar tea plantation visit
  • Evening: Ayurveda massage or local spice market

Total price for this place/per person: Rs.10,000

Special Destinations: Alleppey Backwaters, Munnar Hills, Kovalam Beach

Delhi -3days

Explore India’s capital: a mix of imperial monuments, busy bazaars and modern urban life — ideal for history, food and shopping.

  • Morning: Red Fort & Chandni Chowk (street food walk)
  • Afternoon: Qutub Minar & Lotus Temple
  • Evening: India Gate and Connaught Place / malls

Total price for this place/per person: Rs.6100

Special Destinations: Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate

Himachal Pradesh -5days

Breathe the mountain air — scenic hill stations, snow activities (seasonal) and peaceful ridge walks make this unforgettable.

  • Morning: Shimla Mall Road & Ridge or Kufri (snow activities)
  • Afternoon: Travel to/around Manali — Hidimba Temple, Old Manali
  • Evening: Local food and relax at mountain cafes

Total price for this place/per person: Rs.9600

Special Destinations: Shimla, Manali, Rohtang Pass / Solang Valley
